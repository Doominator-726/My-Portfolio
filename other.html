I have been uploading videos onto Youtube for years. Originally, I used my channel to upload random clips from video games. At one point my channel had over 100 videos. However, I decided to delist all of those videos so I could focus on using my channel to showcase my game development projects. I started a series of videos where I gave updates on my game, Cultus Astrorum, and I also uploaded sections of all of my Pygames. In the process of making these videos I have learned quite a bit about video editing and production. Overall, it has been a nice place for cataloging my work.
